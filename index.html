Jim Cooper
Tennessee's 5th Congressional District
Political Affiliation: Democrat

Biography

Jim Cooper was born and raised in the district he now represents, the metropolitan citiy of Nashville. He attended MBA, a prestigious all boys school in Nashville. In fact, it is the school Dead Poet's Society is based off of. He graduated Harvard Law School in 1980 and was elected to congress in 1982. He has been serving in congress ever since. In his personal life, he has a wife and a few kids.
